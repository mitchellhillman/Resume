Hi!

My Name is Mitchell Hillman and I am a graphic designer, sometimes illustrator, and programmer. I work as a User Interface Designer and Developer with the CFA Institute.

As a designer I strive to create marketing media that speaks the clearest possible message with the most delightful user experience. In pursuit of this goal I seek ways to combine minimal graphic design with a common-sense use of technology.

Also, I believe that succeeding at design requires a firm grasp of production technique. For that reason I am fascinated with the practices of my trade. Being able to build what I concieve is very important to me. Currently I am working a lot with jQuery, SASS, Adobe CC, and Drupal, but I am learning about exciting new tools all the time.

If you would like my help on one of your projects, please feel free to reach out through one of my social networks.
