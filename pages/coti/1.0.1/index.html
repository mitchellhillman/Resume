Church of the Incarnation

Logomark Proposal v1.0.1

This design is a conservative, but not slavish reproduction of the existing concept. It keeps the bell tower its subject and attempts to be recognizable by retaining the same approximate silhouette of the sky and horizon lines. The bell tower itself is redrawn to better represent the actual proportions of the facade while aligning to an internal grid. Simplicity has been emphasized to allow the logo to read at small sizes and reproduce easily in a wide variety of formats.
